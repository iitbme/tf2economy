About this site

What is this?

This site is created to prevent TF2 economy from crashing. Instead of using price values that change frequently, traders can now follow a fixed price for currency items.


Why fix prices?

No one wants key prices to go up and buds or refined value to drop. It is not the fault of community sites that reflect the market value. It is because of the traders that these price changes are happening. Dropping prices creates panic among sellers and they further drop the price ending up in a loop of price drop. Follow these fixed prices to break that loop.
